# Blameless post-incident review (RCA)

**Complete within:** 48 hours of incident resolution
**Participants:** IC, Technical Lead, Scribe, relevant engineers — not the full company
**Format:** Written doc first, then 30-minute review meeting
**Principle:** This process is about systems and processes, not individuals. No blame is assigned.
